ProcessProces

From Voyage Data to Verified Report

Od Voyage Podataka do Verificiranog Izvještaja

Five simple steps — from data entry to THETIS-MRV submission, all automated.

Pet jednostavnih koraka — od unosa podataka do slanja na THETIS-MRV, sve automatizirano.

01

Input Voyage Data

Unesite Voyage Podatke

Fuel, route, cargo, BDN number. Auto-detects EU/non-EU route and ETS factor. Works online and offline.

Gorivo, ruta, teret, BDN broj. Automatski detektira EU/non-EU rutu i ETS faktor. Radi online i offline.

02

Validate & Calculate

Validacija i Izračun

Smart validation checks data. Python service calculates TtW emissions, ETS allowances, and FuelEU GHG intensity.

Pametna validacija provjerava podatke. Python servis izračunava TtW emisije, ETS kvote i FuelEU GHG intenzitet.

03

Generate Reports

Generirajte Izvještaje

Annex II MRV report, regulatory document, and voyage report generated as PDFs. Mandatory review before archiving.

Annex II MRV izvještaj, regulatorni dokument i voyage izvještaj generiraju se kao PDF-ovi. Obavezni pregled prije arhiviranja.

04

Send to Verifier

Pošaljite Verificatoru

Automatic email to Bureau Veritas, DNV or Lloyd's. Upload Document of Compliance when received.

Automatski email Bureau Veritasu, DNV-u ili Lloyd'su. Uploadajte Document of Compliance kada stigne.

05

Submit to THETIS-MRV

Pošaljite na THETIS-MRV

Electronic submission to EMSA portal. THETIS reference number saved. All documents archived for 5 years.

Elektroničko slanje na EMSA portal. THETIS referentni broj pohranjen. Svi dokumenti arhivirani 5 godina.
